On the home screen of the game if the player already has an account signed up then he has to login using his/her user id and passsword and if he/she has no account then they could sign up to make an account. After login player will be directed to the next page in which either he/she could play with a random group of players by clicking on play with random player or he/she could play with computer by clicking play with computer or he/she could play with his/her friends by inserting same game id and number of players. This will direct him/her to the game screen.
game rules :-
	i) Player will die if it will hit the boundary or will hit the trail of any player(including the player himself too).
	ii) On every move of the player it will get 1 point and if it will hit the trail of any other player then that player will get 100 points.
	iii) The last man survived will won the match.
